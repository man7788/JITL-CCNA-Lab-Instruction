# Day 02 Lab - Connecting Devices

## Cable Type

### Copper Straight-Through

1. PCs -> Switches
2. Servers -> Switches
3. Switches -> Routers

### Copper Cross-Over

1. Switches -> Switches
2. Routers -> Routers

> If the distance is less than 100 meters

### Fiber

1. Switches -> Routers
2. Router -> Routers

> If the distance is less more than 100 meters
